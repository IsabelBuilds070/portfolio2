About Me

I'm Isabel, I design, build & figure it out.

I'm a Computer Science student and self-taught web developer based in Lagos, Nigeria 🌍. I specialise in Front-End Development, UI/UX Design and I'm actively expanding into Full Stack development one project at a time.

I started my #100DaysOfCode journey and in just 6 days I've already built and deployed real projects including a live weather app, calculator, Gymn landing page and MovieSearch app with trailer integration. I believe in learning by doing and building in public no waiting until I'm "ready".

Beyond coding I bring real world experience in Customer Service and Tech Support which means I don't just build things, I build things with the user in mind. I understand people, I solve problems and I communicate clearly.

I'm open to remote roles, freelance projects and collaborations worldwide 🌍
